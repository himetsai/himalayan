Who We Are

A home for the Himalayas at Oxford

"The Himalayan Society is a new student organisation that provides a space for those from or interested in the region to learn more about the diverse cultures of the Himalayas. Be it guest lectures, Lhosar celebrations, momo-making events, or language immersion sessions, we hope to see you soon."

Spanning Nepal, Tibet, Bhutan, and the mountainous reaches of northern India, the Himalayan region is home to some of the world's most extraordinary cultures, languages, and traditions. We believe these deserve to be shared, celebrated, and better understood. Oxford is the perfect place to do it.

Whether you grew up in the shadow of the mountains or are simply curious about the world beyond the peaks, you'll find a warm welcome here. No prior knowledge required. Just an open mind.
